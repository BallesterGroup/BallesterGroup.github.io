Welcome to Ballester Group

Our research aims at developing and applying artificial intelligence algorithms to boost the frequency and novelty of biomedical discoveries. We focus on Drug Design and Precision Oncology problems from an applications research perspective. From a methodology research perspective, we are mostly interested in understanding and mitigating the impact of factors such as data bias, high dimensionality or distribution shifts on supervised learning. Among other requirements, here it is important to understand how the relevant datasets are generated, find out how these can be exploited in an optimal manner and investigate which artificial intelligence algorithms are the most suitable for the considered problem instance.

Research Interests
  • AI for Precision Oncology
  • Computational Chemstriy and AI for Drug Design
  • Computational (Virtual) Screening
